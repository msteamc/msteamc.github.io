Make STEM Clear

Welcome to MSC

MSC, Make STEM Clear, is a project to show STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics ) in a visualized and animated way. The main part is the Youtube Channel and the posts in this site. Any topic related with STEM maybe covered. However, there is an order. Topics related with information and communication theory, computer science and the corresponding math background will be covered first.

Interests

  • Math related with engineering students
  • Information and Communication Theory
  • Computer Science and Technology
